Halfsies

Halfsies is a mobile app that helps users easily split payments with friends and keep track of debts. Built with the Flutter SDK and utilizing Firebase as a database, Halfsies is available on both Android and iOS. While attempting to implement the Capital One API, the team encountered challenges and time constraints, but ultimately succeeded in connecting the front end and back end of the app. Through the development process, the team learned valuable skills in app development, patience, and efficient brainstorming.
